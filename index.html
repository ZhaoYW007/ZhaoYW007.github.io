I am a final-year Ph.D. student at CMU, advised by Prof. Phillip B. Gibbons. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University with highest honor in 2021. I also worked as a Research Scientist Intern in Meta in 2023 and 2025, and with Prof. Julian Shun in MIT CSAIL during 2020-2021.

I design faster and more efficient computer systems and architecture. My research focuses on cross-stack co-design for emerging hardware systems spanning three levels: (i) algorithms and data structures, (ii) systems and architectures, and (iii) hardware and circuits. My work aims to develop general and foundational techniques for designing future hardware systems that are both theoretically grounded and practically efficient.

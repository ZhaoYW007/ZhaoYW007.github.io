Bio

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at CMU, advised by Prof. Phillip B. Gibbons. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering in Tsinghua University with highest honor in 2021, where I was advised by Prof. Dan Pei and Prof. Yongpan Liu. I also worked as a Research Scientist Intern in Meta Reality Labs in 2023, and with Prof. Julian Shun in MIT CSAIL during 2020-2021.

Research Interests

My research focuses on algorithm engineering and computer system design for modern and/or future hardware, aiming at bridging the gap between algorithmic theory, and system and architecture design. To be specific, I am recently designing heterogeneous-memory systems and algorithms for databases and AI/ML, featuring principled (i.e., theoretically-guaranteed and practically-efficient) techniques.
